Let's explore Top 3 Genre's in the Bookish World

Top 3 Genre's are : a)Fantasy-The fantasy genre is a type of fiction that features elements that are not real, such as magic, mythology, or life from other worlds.
b)Crime & Mystery-The mystery genre is a genre of fiction that follows a crime (like a murder or a disappearance) from the moment it is committed to the moment it is solved.
c)Non-Fiction- Nonfiction is a broad genre of writing that includes books that are not based on a fictional narrative.
